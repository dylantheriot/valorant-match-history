FAQs


Why did you make this site?

My friend showed me RumbleMikee's project and I thought it was really exciting, so I wanted to try and utilize it in my own way. It was a great learning experience and I really enjoyed all of the responses and feedback I received.


Did you steal my data?

No. I did not steal or track any of your Riot Games data. I'm just a college student who saw the opportunity for a quick, fun project and acted on it.


Why are you taking this down?

Admittedly, this isn't a FAQ but it is a question I'm expecting. I really made this as a fun thing for my friends and I (and got the URL as a gag), and the growth of this site has been tremendously humbling. However, I knew going into it it would be a short lived project. I'm pretty sure Riot plans to release this data in the next VALORANT patch anyways. My life has been consumed by this project for the past few days and I just want to move on to other things.


So why not keep the site running?

I figured Riot was eventually going to take it down, and as the proactive person that I am I just wanted to get ahead of the game! Plus, I'd like to be on their good side as I would love to eventually work for them as a software engineer (*wink wink nudge nudge*).


Any plans for the future?

I've been working on a T-shirt designing website for a small company for awhile. So be on the lookout for that! Otherwise feel free to follow me on YouTube, Twitch (where I had actually streamed myself creating this site), and LinkedIn. You can also learn more about me here.
